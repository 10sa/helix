ix.class

Helper library for loading/getting class information.

Classes are temporary assignments for characters - analogous to a "job" in a faction. For example, you may have a police faction in your schema, and have "police recruit" and "police chief" as different classes in your faction. Anyone can join a class in their faction by default, but you can restrict this as you need with CLASS.CanSwitchTo.
